<p><p>The airplane solenoid valves market comprises primarily two types: 2-way and 3-way solenoid valves. </p><p>2-way solenoid valves direct fluid flow between two ports, efficiently managing single circuits within aircraft systems. In contrast, 3-way solenoid valves can control airflow or liquid in multiple directions, enabling more complex configurations and applications, such as switching or diverting fluid flow. Both types are essential for precise control in aircraft systems, ensuring reliability and performance in various aerospace applications.</p></p>
