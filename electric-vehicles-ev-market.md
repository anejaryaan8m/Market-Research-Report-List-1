<p><p>Electric Vehicles (EVs) encompass various types, including Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), Plug-in Electric Vehicles (PEVs), and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). BEVs run solely on electricity, utilizing large batteries for range. PEVs can plug in to recharge and may combine electric and gasoline power. HEVs, on the other hand, use both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, optimizing fuel efficiency without needing external charging. Each type addresses different consumer needs and contributes to reducing carbon emissions and reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
