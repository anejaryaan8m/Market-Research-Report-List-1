<p><p>The polyurea spray equipment market is primarily segmented into pneumatic and hydraulic spray equipment. Pneumatic spray equipment utilizes compressed air to atomize and apply polyurea, offering versatility and ease of use for smaller applications. In contrast, hydraulic spray equipment relies on high-pressure pumps to deliver a more powerful and consistent spray, making it suitable for large-scale projects that require extensive coverage and durability. Both types cater to different industry needs, influencing their selection based on project scale and requirements.</p></p>
