<p><p>The Common Metal Mask (CMM) market encompasses various types, including Electroplate, Cutting, and Etching. Electroplating involves depositing a metal layer onto a substrate to create precision masks for circuit patterns. Cutting refers to the mechanical shaping of metal masks to specific designs and dimensions for photolithography applications. Etching uses chemical processes to remove material, creating intricate patterns on the metal surface. Together, these techniques cater to the growing demand for high-precision manufacturing in electronics and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
