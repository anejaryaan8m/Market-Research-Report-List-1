<p><p>The car sealed lead-acid battery market primarily features AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize fiberglass mats to absorb the electrolyte, providing excellent vibration resistance and maintenance-free operation, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica-based electrolyte that enhances safety and deep cycling capabilities, making them suitable for applications requiring stable voltage and deeper discharges. Both types serve various automotive needs, balancing performance and longevity.</p></p>
