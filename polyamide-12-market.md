In contrast, ring-opening polymerization typically starts with lactams, leading to a more versatile and controlled polymerization process. Both methods are critical for producing high-performance Polyamide 12, serving various applications in automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, due to their unique properties and advantages.</p></p>
