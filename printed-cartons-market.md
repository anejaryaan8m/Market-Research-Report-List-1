<p><p>The printed cartons market comprises various types of packaging materials, including paperboard, corrugated board, kraft board, coated paper, and liquid board. Paperboard is used for lightweight packaging like folding cartons, while corrugated board offers strength for shipping and storage. Kraft board is known for its durability and resistance, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Coated paper enhances graphics and provides a smooth finish for high-quality prints. Liquid board is designed for containing liquids, often used in beverage packaging. Each type serves specific needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
