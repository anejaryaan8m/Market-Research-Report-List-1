<p><p>The infrared optical filters market is categorized into three main types: White Glass (IRCF), Blue Glass (IRCF), and Resin (IRCF). White Glass filters provide broad spectral transmission, making them suitable for a variety of applications in infrared systems. Blue Glass filters, with their specific absorption characteristics, are used for selective wavelength applications. These filters are crucial in areas like telecommunications, surveillance, and medical imaging, enhancing optical performance and system efficiency.</p></p>
