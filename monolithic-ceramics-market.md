<p><ul><li>Oxides</li><li>Non-oxides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monolithic ceramics market is divided into two main types: oxides and non-oxides. Oxide ceramics, primarily composed of metal oxides like alumina and zirconia, are known for their excellent thermal and chemical stability, making them ideal for applications in electronics and aerospace. Non-oxide ceramics, including carbides, nitrides, and borides, exhibit superior hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for cutting tools and armor applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, emphasizing performance and durability in demanding environments.</p></p>
