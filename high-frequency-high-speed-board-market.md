<p><p>The High Frequency High Speed Board market is categorized into two main types: High Frequency CCL and High Speed CCL. High Frequency CCL (Copper Clad Laminates) are designed for applications requiring minimal signal loss at elevated frequencies, making them ideal for telecommunications and RF circuits. In contrast, High Speed CCL is optimized for minimal signal degradation at higher data rates, ensuring efficient performance in high-speed digital circuits. Both types are essential for advanced electronic devices demanding superior signal integrity.</p></p>
