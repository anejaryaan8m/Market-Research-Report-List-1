<p><ul><li>CVC Catheters</li><li>Implantable Port</li><li>Dialysis Catheters</li><li>PICC Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vascular access market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **CVC Catheters** (Central Venous Catheters) are used for long-term medication delivery and fluid management, often inserted into large veins.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Implantable Ports** provide a durable access point for medication administration, typically for cancer patients.</p><p>3. **Dialysis Catheters** are specialized for patients requiring renal therapy, ensuring efficient blood access.</p><p>4. **PICC Catheters** (Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters) allow for extended intravenous therapy while minimizing complications associated with traditional central lines.</p></p>
