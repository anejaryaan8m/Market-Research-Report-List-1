<p><p>The diesel vehicle exhaust system market is primarily divided into two types: single exhaust systems and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system typically features one exhaust outlet, simplifying design and installation while effectively managing emissions and noise. In contrast, a dual exhaust system has two separate exhaust outlets, which can enhance vehicle performance by allowing better exhaust flow and reducing back pressure. This type is often favored in performance vehicles for improved power and a more aggressive sound profile.</p></p>
