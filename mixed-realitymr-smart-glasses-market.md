<p><p>The Mixed Reality (MR) Smart Glasses market is primarily categorized into two types: All-in-one and Split Type. All-in-one smart glasses integrate all necessary components, including processing, display, and sensors, into a single device. They offer ease of use and portability. In contrast, Split Type smart glasses consist of separate components where processing is handled by an external device, such as a smartphone or computer.
