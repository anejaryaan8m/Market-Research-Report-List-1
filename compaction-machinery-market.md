<p><p>The compaction machinery market is divided into two primary types: heavy compaction machines and light compaction machines. Heavy compaction machines, such as roller compactors and plate compactors, are designed for large-scale projects, providing high levels of density in asphalt and soil for roads, foundations, and large landscaping areas. In contrast, light compaction machines, including rammers and handheld compactors, are suited for smaller, more precise applications like repairing sidewalks or compacting gravel in tight spaces, offering agility and ease of use.</p></p>
