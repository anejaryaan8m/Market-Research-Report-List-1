<p><p>Injection-Blow Moulding (IBM) Systems are a manufacturing process that combines injection moulding and blow moulding techniques to produce hollow plastic items, such as bottles and containers. In this process, a preform is first created using injection moulding, then transferred to a blow mould where it is expanded using air pressure to achieve the desired shape. This method is highly efficient, allowing for precise control over thickness and enhancing surface finish, making it ideal for packaging applications.</p><p>The Injection-Blow Moulding (IBM) Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period. Fully-automatic systems operate independently, minimizing human intervention and achieving high production speeds and consistency, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Semi-automatic systems require some manual input, allowing for flexibility and adaptation in smaller production runs or varied product designs. Both types serve different market needs, with fully-automatic systems focusing on efficiency and output volume, while semi-automatic systems cater to customization and smaller batch requirements.</p></p>
