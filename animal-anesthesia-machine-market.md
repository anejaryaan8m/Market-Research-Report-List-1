<p><p>The animal anesthesia machine market comprises various types designed for veterinary use. Air anesthesia machines utilize ambient air to deliver anesthetic gases, facilitating simpler setups. Once-through anesthesia machines provide a single-use system, ensuring minimal gas waste and easier maintenance. Closed loop type anesthesia machines recycle exhaled gases, optimizing usage and reducing environmental impact while maintaining precise control over anesthetic delivery. Each type caters to specific veterinary needs, enhancing surgical safety and efficiency.</p></p>
