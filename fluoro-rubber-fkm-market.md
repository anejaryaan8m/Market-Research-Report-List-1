<p><p>Fluoro rubber (FKM) is a synthetic elastomer known for its chemical resistance and high-temperature stability. The market is segmented into various types based on their polymer composition: </p><p>- **VD (Vinylidene fluoride)**: Offers excellent chemical resistance and is commonly used in automotive and aerospace applications. </p><p>- **HFP (Hexafluoropropylene)**: Provides flexibility and is suitable for harsh environments. </p><p>- **TFE (Tetrafluoroethylene)**: Known for its thermal stability, often used in industrial applications. </p><p>- **PMVE (Perfluoromethyl vinyl ether)**: Enhances rubber properties, especially for specialized applications. </p><p>- **Other**: Includes various blends and formulations tailored for specific industrial uses.</p></p>
