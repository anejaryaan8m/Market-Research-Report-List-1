<p><p>The air bubble detectors market comprises two primary types: fixed air bubble detectors and adjustable air bubble detectors. Fixed air bubble detectors are designed to detect specific bubble sizes and are typically used in applications with consistent flow conditions. In contrast, adjustable air bubble detectors offer flexibility, allowing users to modify the sensitivity settings to cater to varying bubble sizes in different environments. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and accuracy in medical, industrial, and fluid transport applications.</p></p>
