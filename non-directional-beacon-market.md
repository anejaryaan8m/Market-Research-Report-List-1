<p><p>The Non-directional Beacon (NDB) market comprises two main types: Medium Frequency (MF) and Low Frequency (LF) NDBs. MF NDBs operate in the 280-315 kHz range, providing reliable navigational aids for aviation and marine applications. LF NDBs function at 190-535 kHz, primarily supporting maritime navigation and aiding pilots in low-visibility conditions. Both types enhance situational awareness and guide users in their respective domains, ensuring safety and efficiency in transportation and navigation systems across varied environments.</p></p>
