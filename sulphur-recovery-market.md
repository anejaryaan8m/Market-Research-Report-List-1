<p><p>The sulphur recovery market primarily encompasses three types: the Claus process, which converts hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur and is widely used in refining and natural gas processing; the modified Claus process, which enhances recovery efficiency by integrating additional technologies; and tail gas treatment, which focuses on further processing the residual gases from the Claus process to minimize environmental impact and maximize sulfur recovery. Together, these methods contribute to effective sulphur management and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
